What is a Word Counter?

A word counter is a free online tool that automatically counts the number of words, characters, sentences and paragraphs in any text. Instead of counting manually, simply paste your text and get instant, accurate results.

Our word counter also shows reading time, grade level, and checks your text against social media character limits — making it perfect for writers, students, bloggers, and SEO professionals.

When Should You Use a Word Counter?

  • Students: Meet minimum word counts for essays, dissertations, and assignments.
  • Bloggers: Hit the ideal SEO word count for your blog posts (1,200–2,000 words).
  • Social Media: Make sure your tweets, captions, and meta descriptions fit character limits.
  • Freelance Writers: Track word count for client deliverables and invoicing.
  • Authors: Monitor chapter and manuscript length during writing sessions.

How is Reading Time Calculated?

Reading time is calculated based on the average adult reading speed of 200 words per minute. A 1,000-word article takes approximately 5 minutes to read. This metric is especially useful for blog posts and news articles, where knowing the read time helps readers decide whether to engage.

What is Grade Level?

The grade level score is based on the Flesch-Kincaid readability formula. It estimates which school grade level is needed to easily understand your text. A grade level of 8 means an 8th-grade student can read it. Most popular websites and newspapers target a grade level of 6–8 for maximum reach.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the word counter work? +
Our word counter processes your text directly in your browser using JavaScript. When you type or paste text, it splits the content by spaces to count words, counts every character for the character count, detects sentence-ending punctuation for sentences, and looks for line breaks for paragraphs. Everything happens instantly with no server involved — your text never leaves your device.
Does character count include spaces? +
Yes — the "Characters" count includes spaces. The "No Spaces" count shows only letters, numbers and punctuation without spaces. Use the "No Spaces" count for platforms that count characters without spaces, and the full character count for platforms like Twitter that count spaces as characters.
Is this word counter free? +
Yes, completely free. No account, no subscription, no download required. Simply paste your text and get results instantly. There are no limits on how much text you can count.
Is my text stored or shared? +
No. All text processing happens locally in your browser. Your text is never sent to any server and we never store, save or share anything you type. You can verify this by using the tool while disconnected from the internet — it still works perfectly.
What is the ideal word count for SEO blog posts? +
For SEO purposes, blog posts should typically be between 1,200 and 2,000 words. Long-form content above 2,000 words tends to rank higher on Google because it covers topics more thoroughly. However, content quality matters more than length — every word should add value for the reader.
How many words is a 5-minute read? +
At the average reading speed of 200 words per minute, a 5-minute read is approximately 1,000 words. A 10-minute read is around 2,000 words. Our tool automatically calculates reading time for any text you paste in.
